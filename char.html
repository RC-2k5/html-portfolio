Top 7 Most Iconic Mortal Kombat Characters

This is a list of top 7 most iconic MK characters of all time

3.Sub Zero

The arch rival of Scorpion and the second most iconic ninja who is a pallette swap to Scorpion (blue), he is known freezing the opponents and ripping out their spines
